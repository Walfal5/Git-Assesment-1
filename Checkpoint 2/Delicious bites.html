Menu

Appetizers

  • Bruschetta - Toasted bread topped with fresh tomatoes, basil, and a drizzle of balsamic glaze.
  • Stuffed Mushrooms - Button mushrooms filled with a creamy blend of cheese, herbs, and garlic.
  • Chicken Wings - Crispy wings tossed in your choice of spicy buffalo or sweet barbecue sauce.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon - Perfectly grilled salmon served with a lemon butter sauce and roasted vegetables.
  • Spaghetti Carbonara - A classic Italian pasta dish with pancetta, eggs, Parmesan, and black pepper.
  • BBQ Ribs - Slow-cooked ribs smothered in tangy barbecue sauce, served with coleslaw and fries.

Desserts

  • Tiramisu - A rich and creamy coffee-flavored Italian dessert with layers of mascarpone and cocoa.
  • Chocolate Lava Cake - Warm chocolate cake with a gooey molten center, served with vanilla ice cream.
  • Cheesecake - Classic cheesecake with a buttery graham cracker crust and a choice of fruit topping.
